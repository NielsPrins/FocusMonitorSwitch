# FocusMonitorSwitch

FocusMonitorSwitch automatically switches focus to the frontmost app on a monitor when you move your mouse to it.

macOS does not do this by default, moving your cursor to another screen does not bring that screen's app into focus until you click.
FocusMonitorSwitch removes that extra click.

It runs silently in the background with no Dock icon or menu bar item.
